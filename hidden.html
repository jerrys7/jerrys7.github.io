Making geographical education more accessible to high school students

The Canadian Geographic Workshops are a series endorsed by Canadian Geography Education

Taught by former Team Canada Alumni at the International Geography Olympiad

We are former members of the 2018 and 2019 iterations of Team Canada and have placed very highly around students in the world. We are as enthusiastic about geography as we are about education and we hope our experiences will help motivate your journey into geography.
